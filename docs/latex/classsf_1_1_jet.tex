\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\mbox{\hyperlink{classsf_1_1_jet}{Jet}} velocity field class. 

Class implements a velocity field coming from a water jet. The flow velocity is specified at the centre of the jet outlet. The closer to the outlet boundary the slower the flow (zero at boudary).
