\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\mbox{\hyperlink{classsf_1_1_stream}{Stream}} (current) velocity field class. 

Class implements a velocity field in a shape of a tube along a Hermite spline, with variable diameter. The flow velocity is specified at the centre of the beginning of the tube (tanget to spline). The closer to the boundary the slower the flow (zero at boudary).
